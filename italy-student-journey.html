Italy Student Visa Journey

Step-by-step roadmap for Pakistani students applying for an Italian National Long-Stay Visa for Study (Type D) — from university admission and pre-enrolment through arriving in Italy and converting to a Permesso di Soggiorno.

Disclaimer: Informational guidance only. Italian visa rules and pre-enrolment procedures change each academic year. Always confirm current requirements with the Italian Embassy in Islamabad and at vistoperitalia.esteri.it before taking any action.
🇮🇹
Italian National Long-Stay Visa for Study (Visto Nazionale Tipo D per Studio)
Pakistani students require an Italian National Long-Stay Visa for Study (Type D) for study programmes lasting more than 90 days. Many Italian universities require a formal pre-enrolment step submitted via the Italian Embassy or through the official Italian university portals before the visa application. After arrival, you must apply for a Permesso di Soggiorno per Studio (Residence Permit for Study) within 8 working days. Confirm current procedures at vistoperitalia.esteri.it and at the Italian Embassy in Islamabad.
