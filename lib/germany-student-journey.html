Germany Student Visa Journey

Step-by-step roadmap for Pakistani students applying for a German National Visa for study purposes — from university admission through arriving in Germany and registering your residence permit.

Disclaimer: Informational guidance only. German visa regulations can change. Always confirm current requirements with the German Embassy Islamabad and the Federal Foreign Office before taking any action.
🇩🇪
German National Visa (Section 16b AufenthG)
Pakistani students require a German National Visa for study purposes (issued under Section 16b of the German Residence Act), not a Schengen short-stay visa. After arrival, you must convert this to a Residence Permit for Study at the local Foreigners' Authority (Ausländerbehörde). Applications are submitted at the German Embassy in Islamabad (or consulate if applicable). Visit islamabad.diplo.de for official requirements.
