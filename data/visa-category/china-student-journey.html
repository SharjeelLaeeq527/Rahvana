China Student Visa Journey

Step-by-step roadmap for Pakistani students applying for a Chinese student visa (X1 or X2) — from university admission and required documents through arriving in China and completing residence permit registration.

Disclaimer: Informational guidance only. Chinese visa policies and requirements can change. Always confirm current requirements with the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan and the China Visa Application Service Center before taking any action.
🇨🇳
X1 vs X2 Student Visa
X1 visa is for students whose course in China is longer than 180 days. After entry, X1 holders must apply for a Residence Permit for Study at the local Public Security Bureau (PSB) within 30 days of arrival. X2 visa is for short-term study of 180 days or less and does not require conversion to a residence permit. Confirm the correct visa type for your program with the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan.
