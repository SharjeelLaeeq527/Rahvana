Student Visa – Subclass 500
The Australian Student visa (Subclass 500) allows you to study a full-time registered course at an Australian education provider. You must have a Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) from a registered provider and meet the Genuine Student requirement. Applications are lodged online through ImmiAccount on the Home Affairs website. Review all current requirements at immi.homeaffairs.gov.au.
